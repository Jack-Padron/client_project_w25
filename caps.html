Counseling and Psychological Services

Our Mission

University Health & Counseling (UHC) supports the health and well-being of the campus community. CAPS is committed to providing high-quality mental health services to empower students, support academic success, and foster a sense of connection and resilience within the Michigan community.

Services We Offer

  • Individual Peer Counseling (IPC): A space for students to vent and receive support from trained peers.
  • Uwill Teletherapy: Access video, phone, chat, and messaging sessions with licensed mental health professionals for free.
  • Togetherall: An anonymous online peer-support tool where students can share and support each other in a safe, moderated community.
  • Stress Less Workshops: Tools and strategies to help manage stress effectively.

Key Resources

CAPS is proud to offer both in-person and virtual services, ensuring accessibility for all students. For those requiring telecounseling privacy, CAPS provides private spaces upon request. Visit our front desk on the 4th floor of the Michigan Union for assistance.

Support and Inclusivity

CAPS is committed to creating a welcoming and affirming environment for all students, celebrating diversity and promoting social justice. We stand against discrimination and harassment, working to ensure that every student feels valued and supported.
